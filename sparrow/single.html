ACM-ICPC

Jan 22, 2018 / Competitive

Greetings!

NJACK would like to congratulate ButterRoti, a team from IIT Patna, who won the runners up position at the ACM ICPC Kolkata-Kanpur India Regionals and got selected for the World Finals to be held in Beijing, China in April. It is a great feat to be one of the 8 teams out of 3200 teams that participated in the online round. Being fellow members of NJACK, we would like to wish ButterRoti good luck and hope they will give their all in the World Finals.
